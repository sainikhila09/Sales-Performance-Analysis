# 📈 Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Alfido Tech Data Analytics Internship** through the **InternSpark** platform.

In this project, I analyzed sales data using Python to understand sales trends across different regions, product categories, and time periods. The analysis helped identify high-performing products, compare regional sales performance, and generate business recommendations based on the available data.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and preprocess the sales dataset.
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Analyze sales across regions and product categories.
- Calculate key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Source:** Kaggle

The dataset contains sales records with details such as order information, regions, product categories, customer segments, and sales values.

## 📊 Project Workflow

The following steps were performed during the analysis:

## 📈 Visualizations

The notebook includes the following visualizations:

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Sales varied across different regions and product categories.
- Some products generated significantly higher revenue than others.
- Monthly sales trends showed changes in customer purchasing patterns.
- KPIs such as Total Revenue, Average Order Value, and Total Orders provided a clear overview of business performance.
- The visualizations made it easier to identify high-performing areas and potential opportunities for improvement.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
- Promote best-selling products through targeted campaigns.
- Monitor monthly sales trends to improve planning.
- Improve sales strategies for lower-performing categories.
- Use KPI tracking for regular business performance evaluation.
